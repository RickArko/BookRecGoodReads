# Offline Evaluation Results

_Generated by `evaluate.py`. Reproducible with the seed and parameters below._

## Setup

- Catalog size: **50,000** books
- Sampled users: **500** (target 500, min interactions 10)
- Random seed: `42`
- Cutoffs evaluated: K=5, K=10, K=20

## Methodology

For each sampled user we pick one of their interacted books at random as the **seed** query and treat the user's other interacted books as held-out **positives**. The recommender ranks the catalog from the seed; the top-K is scored against the positives.

**Caveat**: each recommender is fit on the full interaction matrix, including the held-out positives. Reported numbers are therefore a small upper bound on what production performance would look like with strict masking. Comparisons *between* recommenders remain valid because all of them see the same training data.

## Metrics

- **Precision@K**: fraction of the top-K that are relevant.
- **Recall@K**: fraction of relevant items captured in the top-K.
- **NDCG@K**: rank-aware metric (binary relevance).
- **MAP@K**: mean of average-precision across users.
- **Hit-Rate@K**: probability that at least one relevant item lands in the top-K.
- **Diversity**: mean pairwise cosine *distance* among the top-K recs in content-feature space.
- **Coverage**: fraction of catalog reached across all rec lists.
